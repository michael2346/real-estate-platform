Our Commission Structure

Transparent pricing for buyers to connect with property owners

How Our Commission Works

To ensure serious inquiries and protect property owners, buyers pay a small commission fee to access owner contact information:

  • Properties under ₦5 Million: ₦5,000 commission fee
  • Properties ₦5 Million - ₦20 Million: ₦10,000 commission fee
  • Properties above ₦20 Million: ₦20,000 commission fee

Note: This fee is only charged when you request to contact the property owner. Browsing listings is completely FREE!
