Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HomeConnect

How does the commission system work?

Buyers pay a small commission fee to unlock owner contact details. This fee varies based on property price: ₦5,000 for properties under ₦5M, ₦10,000 for ₦5M-₦20M, and ₦20,000 for properties above ₦20M.

Is it free to list my property?

Yes! Listing your property on HomeConnect is completely FREE. We only charge buyers a commission fee to contact you, ensuring you get serious inquiries only.

How long does it take for my listing to go live?

All listings are reviewed within 24 hours. Once approved, your property will be visible to all potential buyers on our platform.

Can I edit or remove my listing?

Yes, you can edit or remove your listing at any time by logging into your account and accessing your dashboard.
